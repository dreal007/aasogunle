About Us

A.A. Sogunle and Associates is a firm of legal practitioners incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act, Laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria

The Law firm under the leadership of its Principal Partner, Adebisi Adekunle Sogunle Esq. is a fully integrated and multi-dimensional law office, with Practitioners/Solicitors providing legal services to a highly diversified client base.

Our practice areas encompass the full range of Corporate and Commercial Legal Services, including Banking; Capital Market; Corporate Services; Corporate Governance and Compliance; Employment, Immigration; Financial Institutions Advisory; Intellectual Property; Litigation; Real Estate, Arbitration and Dispute Resolution.

Our Lawyers have decades of experience and have appeared before several courts and tribunals across Nigeria at court of first instance and appellate levels. With different strengths and backgrounds, they often collaborate and work together toward getting results.

The firm’s history and vast professional experience for more than 15 years to clients in major industries, international business, and the corporate world is a distinct advantage in Nigeria's rapidly changing and evolving market. The firm's dedicated and highly skilled professionals are focused on client satisfaction through providing prompt and valuable solutions to their legal matters.

Providing highly skilled and innovative legal solutions to individuals and businesses.
