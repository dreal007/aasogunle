The Firm

A.A. Sogunle and Associates is a firm of legal practitioners incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act, Laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. The Law firm under the leadership of its Principal Partner, Adebisi Adekunle Sogunle Esq. is a fully integrated and multi-dimensional law office, with Practitioners/Solicitors providing legal services to a highly diversified client base.
